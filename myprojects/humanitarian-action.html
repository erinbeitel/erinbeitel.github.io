Your Mission: Feed the Village and Evade the Rebels

July 11, 2015

A refuge village is in danger because rebels have cut off their food supply. As a Navy Seal aid worker, you are specially trained for this situation. Evade the rebels and deliver food to the village. Use your evasive Seal skills to stay out of range of the rebel camps. The rebels will steal food if you get too close. Your mission is to deliver enough food to feed the refuges.
