Margins, Boarders and Padding

Phase 0, Week 2

May 30th, 2015

Margins define how much space exists around an element. The CSS format for defining the margin looks something like this: "margin: top right bottom left".

Borders are the lines that surround an element, inside of the margin. They can have different thicknesses, and they can also have a radius - when a radius is defined the corners of the element are curved.

Padding is the space between the text of an element and the border. Like margins, padding can be defined as so: "padding: top right bottom left".
